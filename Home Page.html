Welcome to SNSVM.....

Sant Nandlal Smriti Vidya Mandir,senior secondary school, is a progressive school with
a contemporary approach. It is a temple of education where emphasis is given on complete
development of a child- mentally, physically and spiritually to enable him to become a
successful and respectable citizen of the world and develop a vibrant society.

It endeavors to extend all possible opportunities to children to participate in learning
through doing and offering conceptual clarity through illustrative presentation and
theme based displays, making learning fun. Sant Nandlal Smriti Vidya Mandir has
emerged as one of the best school in Ghatsila with various infrastructural facilities,
innovative & quality learning methods, it has evolved.

Mankind's highest destiny is to attain perfection, and for this he needs a heart full of bliss,
wisdom full of latest knowledge and healthy body to perform.” Sant Nandlal Smiriti Vidya
Mandir is an effort to translate the above vision into reality.

Today the school is recognized as one of the best schools in Ghatsila sub division, with
modern teaching facilities & a team of dedicated & committed staff with a strength
of over 1400 students. The school is Affiliated to CBSE board vide letter no.
CBSE/ Aff./3430151(Ex-00534-0809)with co-educational facilities up to +2 standard
with Commerce & Science.

PRINCIPAL'S DESK

“Where the mind is without fear and the head is held high”


We at SNSVM have a primary goal to help students grow academically, socially and morally. We respect the differences of each student’s personality, learning style and abilities. Each student is unique in his/her own way and needs a safe, caring, and challenging atmosphere to grow and mature emotionally, intellectually, physically, and socially. We enable each student to grow to become a life-long learner and a responsible global citizen. It is important for students to develop self-esteem, sensitivity, creativity, and respect for others as well as improve upon their critical thinking , communication, problem solving, and research skills while learning how to work collaboratively with others.
