Activities & Facilities

Sant Nandlal Smiriti Vidya Mandir is a progressive school with a contemporary approach. It is a temple of education where emphasis is given on complete development of a child mentally, physically and spiritually to enable him to become a successful and respectable citizen of the world and develop a vibrant society.


Infrastructure

Bright, neat, Spacious and airy classrooms are provided in accordance with the age group of the children to facilitate effective curriculum transaction. There are approximately 40 children in each class. There are soft boards for each class where children put up their art, crafts, project charts etc.
